The New Expeditionary Society

discusses and invents mechanics.

A mechanic is a novel demonstration of an elegant principle. It is often a game, work of art, or technical demo, but can take many forms. So far the mechanics I have come up with relate to language, but others might be very different and still interesting. Existing mechanics I like include chess, go, soccer, The Library of Babel, and mafia.


golf, iterative directionless search of semantic neighbors .
